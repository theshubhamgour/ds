Demon Slayer Future Updates: Upcoming Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga & Anime

Warning: SPOILERS ahead for Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba #205

Demon Slayer (or Kimetsu no Yaiba) was a popular serialized manga series that also spawned a well-received anime series. The story, about a boy named Tanjiro Kamado who decides to slay demons after they kill his family and turn his sister Nezuko into a demon herself, has thrilled both its original Japanese audience and many other manga fans around the world. After 205 chapters, however, the Demon Slayer manga has ended, leaving fans wondering – what’s next? Are there any new stories that can be told about this saga – or will it continue on in other forms of media? Let’s explore some of the announcements and possibilities. START NOW RELATED: 10 Anime To Watch If You Love Demon Slayer Demon Slayer: The Manga On May 17, 2020, the final chapter of the Demon Slayer manga was released, bringing the saga to a definitive close. The last chapter actually features a time jump that reveals many of the popular characters who died fighting the demons were reincarnated as their descendants. Readers get to see their favorite characters finally living the happy, peaceful lives they always wanted in modern-day Japan, giving the dark and frequently painful story of Demon Slayer a happy ending. While other manga have had spin-offs and sequels after ending the original saga, Demon Slayer appears to have granted its characters a definitive end within its manga story. This does not mean, however, that fans have seen the last of these characters, as several other Demon Slayer projects are still in the works. The Demon Slayer Movie The Demon Slayer movie, Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train, has a Japan release date of October 16, 2020. The Japanese anime film will be a direct sequel to the 2019 anime series which finished its first season on September 28, 2019 (in Japan) and May 2, 2020 (in the U.S.). As such, both the Japanese and English voice casts will be reprising their roles for the anime film. The film itself will take place onboard a train where Tanjiro and his friends are investigating several disappearances. Ultimately, they find Enmu, one of the Twelve Kizuki who has a trap for the Demon Slayer Corps. Demon Slayer: The Anime Series With Season 1 having wrapped up, Demon Slayer fans are wondering if the popular anime series will get a second season. Considering the popularity of the manga series – and the fact that the anime has yet to adapt all the stories from the saga yet – a second season is certainly possible. However, no official announcement has been made yet concerning Demon Slayer Season 2 from Studio Ufotable. As of now, fans will simply have to wait in anticipation for the movie before looking forward to new seasons of the anime series. Other Demon Slayer Media Aside from manga, anime series, and movies, Demon Slayer will continue to live on in additional media. A Demon Slayer PlayStation 4 game has been announced for 2021 which will let the player “become Tanjiro Kamado” in a competitive demon slaying game. Additional Demon Slayer Android and Apple iOS games are also in development. With all of these new avenues to experience Demon Slayer being explored, it’s very unlikely that fans will be wanting for any new content or entertainment in the near future.
